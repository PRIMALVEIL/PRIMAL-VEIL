Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for PRIMAL VEIL

Last updated: 27/09/2025

The information we collect

PRIMAL VEIL may temporarily process voice data during gameplay for features such as multiplayer communication. We do not automatically record or download any audio from the players. Recordings may only be created by players themselves if they choose to report inappropriate behavior (e.g., harassment or offensive language) through our community Discord server.

How we use the information

  • To enable in-game voice chat between players to allow communication.
  • To allow players to record and submit reports of misconduct at their own discretion.
  • To review reports for community safety and enforcement. Misconduct is not tolerated.

Data storage

Audio recordings are not collected or stored by PRIMAL VEIL directly. If a player submits a report, the recording is shared through our Discord community server. In that case, the storage and processing of the recording is handled according to Discord’s Privacy Policy. We do not share recordings with third parties outside of moderation needs.

Children’s Privacy

PRIMAL VEIL is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
